Planting Seeds to
Create a
Sustainable Future

We work to provide aid to Syrians affected by violence, poverty, hunger, and
displacement. The volatile situation in Syria has created a dire need for healthcare
protection, and shelter, among other basic necessities. SRD works to meet these needs
in Syria and neighboring countries through comprehensive and integrated programs.

Take your Donation Further

In 2021, 96.06% of our total revenue supported programs for health, protection, nutrition & shelter, directly benefiting internally displaced and refugee Syrians.
